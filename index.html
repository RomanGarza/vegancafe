about us

Our concept was born out of a trip to the city of Zacatecas where we were inspired by so many veggie dishes with such flavor and love. We wanted to bring a modern take on Mexican culture and food to Miami. While the focus is on our large taco selection, we offer many other Mexican staples with a twist. We’ve worked hard to bring you a menu and an overall experience that will have you longing to come back again and again. During a trip to the city of Zacatecas everyone was unsure about what vegan eats would be available. Roberto grew up in Zacatecas and even he was surprised at the ever growing plethora of plant-based options. The trip got everyone excited about bringing the soul of Zacatecas to the heart of Miami, Florida in a way that no one else was doing. Then it all unfolded from there.
